Towers of Hanoi: Quick Overview

Goal:

Move all the discs from the leftmost peg to the rightmost peg.

Rules:

  1. Only one disc can be moved at a time.
  2. A disc can only be moved to the top of another peg if it is smaller than the disc already there.
  3. Only the top disc of any peg can be moved.

Steps:

  1. Start with all discs on the leftmost peg, arranged largest at the bottom to smallest at the top.
  2. Move the discs one at a time, following the rules.
  3. Aim to transfer the entire stack to the rightmost peg with the fewest moves possible.

Tip: For an even number of discs, make the first move to the middle peg. For an odd number of discs, make the first move to the rightmost peg.
